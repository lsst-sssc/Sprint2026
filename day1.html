Day 1 (Feb 18)

Location: Canada Room and Council Chamber, Lanyon Building ( Floor Map)

8:40 am GMT - (Optional) Coffee/Tea/Water available in the Canada Room

9:00 am GMT Welcome - Prof Marty Gregg (Head of the School of Maths and Physics; QUB)

9:10 am GMT Kick-off, Announcements,and Introductions - Colin Chandler (UW) and Meg Schwamb (QUB)

9:30 am GMT Current State of Rubin Observatory Commissioning, Rubin Solar System Processing Update, and Overview of Available Rubin Solar System data - Mario Jurić (UW/Rubin Observatory)

11:00 am GMT Break (Refreshments provided)

11:30 am GMT Lightning Talks

1:00 pm GMT Lunch (provided)

2:00pm GMT - Software Demos (12 minute presentations with a 15 minutes each including Q+A session)

  • Hungary in-kind contribution (Developer Pool Time) - Solar System Object Forced Photometry Tool - Laszlo Szigeti (ELTE Gothard Observatory) and Gyula M. Szabó (ELTE Gothard Astrophysical Observatory)
  • Sorcha - Meg Schwamb (QUB) and Layup Matt Holman (Harvard | Smithsonian CfA)
  • Fink Broker Tools - Benoit Carry (Lagrange, Observatoire de la Côte d'Azur) and Julien Peloton (CNRS/IJCLab)
  • UK in-kind contribution - Adler - Jamie Robinson (University of Edinburgh) and Colin Snodgrass (University of Edinburgh)

3:00 pm GMT Pitch Brainstorming

3:30 pm GMT Break (Refreshments provided)

4:00 pm GMT Rubin Science Platform DP1 (Data Preview 1) image cutout retrieval tutorial - remote talk - Christina Williams (Rubin Observatory/NOIRLab)

4:30 pm GMT Project Pitches

Location: South Dining Hall

5- 7:00pm GMT Optional Project Work (Divide into project groups and work on pitched projects) or Optional discussions or Hang out Before Dinner Arrives

6:00pm -7:30pm GMT Dinner - Orto Pizza Delivered at 6pm
